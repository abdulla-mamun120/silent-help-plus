About Silent Help Plus

Built by Students, for Students

University life comes with unique challenges - academic struggles, financial constraints, limited mentorship, and difficulty accessing quality study materials.

Our platform provides a safe, anonymous space where you can ask questions without judgment, share and access study resources, discover scholarships and job opportunities, connect with mentors, and report serious issues.

With department-wise content filtering and university-wide access to opportunities, we ensure you get relevant information without being overwhelmed.
