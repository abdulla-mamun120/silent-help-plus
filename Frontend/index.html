About Silent Help Plus

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the platform

How does anonymous Q&A work?
When you post a question, the system generates a unique anonymous ID (like Anon_CS_2431) shown to other users instead of your real name. Admins can only trace identity in case of serious policy violations.
Can I upload study materials?
Yes! Click "Upload Resource", select your file, add subject, semester, and description. Your upload goes to admin for approval. Once approved, it becomes available to all students in your department.
Are scholarships available to all departments?
Absolutely! Scholarships and jobs are university-wide resources accessible to all students regardless of department. The system auto-removes expired opportunities.
What is department filtering?
Academic features (Q&A, study resources) are automatically filtered by your department. You'll only see questions and materials relevant to your courses.
How do I become a mentor?
Go to the Mentorship section and click "Register as Mentor". Fill in your expertise areas, availability, and a brief bio. Mentees can search and send connection requests.
Is book exchange really free?
The platform is free to use! List books at whatever price you choose. No platform fees or commissions. You keep 100% of your earnings!
How to report a serious issue?
Click "Report Issue", select report type, provide description. Your reporter ID remains anonymous. Admin reviews all reports and investigates serious cases.
Can admin see my real identity?
Under normal circumstances, no. For serious policy violations, admin can trace IDs - but only with documented reason and full audit trail logging.
